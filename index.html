About Me

Md Omar Faruqe is currently serving as Research Assistant as well as doing PhD in signal processing research field at Electrical & Computer Engineering Department, Concordia University, Canada. He is also working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh where he is a member of the Image Processing research group. Before joining at Rajshahi University, he worked for BRAC University, Bangladesh. His role was a Lecturer in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of the same University. His current research interests include signal processing, image processing, and Machine learning. He has published more than ten technical articles in some well recognized conferences and journals.
Mr. Faruqe received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the University of Rajshahi, Rajshahi, Bangladesh, in 2005 and 2006 respectively. He received several awards including National Science and Technology Fellowship for M.Phil. Research in 2012-2013, National Science and Information & Communication Technology Fellowship for M.Sc. Research in 2007-2008, University Merit (Honors) Scholarship in 2008, Bangladesh Computer Council Internship scholarship, in 2007, University Merit Scholarship for the period 2002-2005.
Besides his research career, he also sounds in computer programming. He is very good in different types of programming language including Java: J2SE, J2ME, J2EE, Android, PHP, Matlab, etc. He is expert in desktop software development as well as web based software development and website design.
